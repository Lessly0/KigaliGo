Plan Your Trip

Ready to explore Kigali? Here are some tips to help you plan your visit:

Category Details
Visa Most visitors can obtain a visa on arrival or apply online for an e-visa.
Currency Rwandan Franc (RWF) is the local currency. Credit cards are accepted in many places.
Language Kinyarwanda, French, and English are widely spoken.
Health Recommended vaccinations include Yellow Fever, Hepatitis A, and Typhoid. Malaria prophylaxis is advised.
